# Q2–Q3 2026 ASO Recovery Plan

**Created:** May 14, 2026 · **Revised:** May 18, 2026
**Status:** Active
**Owner:** Marketing

---

## Context

App Store installs dropped from a Sep 2024 peak of ~29,000/mo to ~860/mo (97% decline). Three stacked failures:

1. **App Store visibility broke late August 2025.** Competitors iterated their listings (Khan Academy Kids Aug 27, HOMER Aug 25); Zoog's listing stood frozen — zero ASO-relevant metadata changes in 12 months. Apple's algorithm reranked.
2. **Long-tail keyword universe collapsed late March / early April 2026.** Ranked keywords dropped from ~120 to ~35. Likely tied to Apple's March 2026 search ads expansion.
3. **Meta paused January 2026.** Install mix shifted to organic; sub CR compressed from Meta's 9.4% to organic's 4.4%.

4.9★ rating is intact — the moat to lean on. The listing isn't broken because we broke it; it's broken because we sat on it while competitors iterated.

**Strategy:** match the cadence — actively refresh metadata, build CPPs, ship In-App Events, submit featuring nominations. ASO is the dominant install channel post-Meta-pause (~77% of installs).
